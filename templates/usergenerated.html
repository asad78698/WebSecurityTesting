Learn How To Use Web Security Tool In 3 Steps

Step 1

First, Login the account from clicking the button availabe above on above page. If you do not have an account create one by clicking register button on the login page

Step 2

Once Logged in, Grab the URL of your werbsite that you want to scan. Enter the URL in box and click on scan

Step 3

Wait for the scan to complete. Once completed the scan result will be visible to you. If your site contain malware then you will also see prevention details
